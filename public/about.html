Built to rank the games that matter.

Rankr is our project for our Engineering Design course at Texas Tech University. The goal was to learn to design like an engineer. We started by coming up with the idea for this site, then interviewed our fellow students as well as gamers we personally knew. The insight we gathered was used to create a paper wireframe, then a high-fidelity Figma prototype, and finally the real thing. We're glad to host a community of gamers — both casual and hardcore — to share opinions on games and find new ones to play. We hope you enjoy using our site as much as we enjoyed making it.
